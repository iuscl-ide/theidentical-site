Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-09

1. Introduction

The Identical ("the app") is a casual puzzle game designed to challenge visual perception and attention. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how information is handled.

2. Data Collection by the App

The Identical does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly.

  • No user accounts are required
  • No names, email addresses, or identifiers are collected
  • No access to location, contacts, camera, or microphone
  • No gameplay data is transmitted or stored externally

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (such as device type and operating system)

This data is used solely for advertising purposes and is processed in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children's Privacy

The app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through third-party services, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Data Security

Since the app does not collect personal data directly, no personal information is stored or processed by us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
